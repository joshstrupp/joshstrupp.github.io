The Rival

An online newspaper I started

The Rival, LLC is an independent student-run online publication that features daily news, opinion, and satire about the George Washington University, the DC metro area, and college culture. Check out the site at RivalDC.com

Our Numbers

1 site. 6 editors. 35 writers. 93,000 users.

Since its inception in March 2014, The Rival has grown into a staff of nearly 50 writers, editors, managers, business developers, and marketers. We've racked up 350,000 page views from 93,000 unique users.

My role

C.O.O.

I have strategic and operational responsibility over internal editorial operations and business development. I also make creative and logistical decisions regarding web design, site functionality, digital strategy, and content. The VP of Business Development and the Editor in Chief report to me.

BizPlan

Semi-Finalists

In December of 2014 we entered into the GW Business Plan Competition, which has grown into one of the biggest in the nation. We were selected from 120 teams as semi-finalists. Our plan details a Rival platform that enables students to consume news, share ideas, and exchange goods or services. The plan comprehensively outlines an advertising and eCommerce strategy, considers all R&D, SG&A, and marketing costs, and a strategy to expand to other unviversities. Finalists decided March 17. Wish us luck!
